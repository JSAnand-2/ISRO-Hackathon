# ISRO-Hackathon

This project was developed for the Bharatiya Antariksh Hackathon, where our team built a geospatial AI/ML-based system integrated with physics-informed decision-making. The platform is designed to identify urban heat stress hotspots, quantify the key drivers of urban heating, and generate optimized, scenario-based cooling interventions to mitigate heat impacts.

My Contributions
Machine Learning Pipeline: Developed and trained a Random Forest Regressor to analyze urban heating drivers and predict heat stress hotspots.

Collaborative Development: Partnered with team members who spearheaded the data collection, preprocessing, and geospatial data engineering pipelines.
